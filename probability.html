Probability Game

Press A or D to begin. Inspired by Aarsons Oracle, this "game" uses Markov Chains and N-Grams to predict the user's next input. The goal is to be under 50% after a 100 button presses. Try to be as random as possible - do you have free will, or will a simple algorithm predict your next move? Both algorithms are essentially Markov Chains, but the N-Gram has lower memory and thus is more inaccurate in most cases.
