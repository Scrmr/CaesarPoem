Probability Game 2

Press A or D to begin.

Inspired by Aarsons Oracle, this "game" uses a Q-learning algorithm to predict what you'll press next.

The goal is to have the algorithm's prediction accuracy be around 50% after at least 100 button presses (the more you press, the more accurate the algorithm becomes). Try to be as random as possible - do you have free will, or will a simple algorithm predict your next move?
